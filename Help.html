How to use SIM Database?

Live Tracker: You just have to enter the number of any person, the tool will provide you the proper details about the person (i.e Location, Service provider/SIM company, Area(Where he/she is), Time zone on Mobile, Connected to the Internet or not).

SIM Database:It will provide you the ownership details of the numbers which are registered before 2021. You just have to enter the number and click on search, it'll provide you the following details of number (Owners Name, CNIC, Address).

CNIC info: It will provide you the details on any CNIC number (i.e Name of person, Mobile number, Addess). You just have to enter the CNIC and click on search.

How to use card maker?

The process of making all cards are almost similar. You just have to fill the form and info that has been asked to fill. After that click on submit and wait for a while. The download button will appear click on it, Your card is ready.

How to use Vehicle Database?

It will provide you the Vehicle ownership details (i.e Name of the owner, Mobile number, Address). You just have to select your province and enter the Vehicles registration number (Number plate number) and model if it has written on number plate.
